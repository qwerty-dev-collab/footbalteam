About Tottenham Hotspur

Tottenham Hotspur Football Club, commonly referred to as Spurs, is a professional football club based in Tottenham, London. Founded in 1882, the club has a rich history of success, including winning multiple league titles, FA Cups, and European trophies. Known for their attacking style of play and passionate fan base, Tottenham remains one of the most popular and respected teams in English football.

  • Stadium: Tottenham Hotspur Stadium

Tottenham has been a part of English football history for over a century, with passionate fans supporting their journey through triumphs and challenges.
